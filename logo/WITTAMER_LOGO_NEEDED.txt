Wittamer Logo Required
=======================

Please add the Wittamer logo file as:
  wittamer.jpg

The logo should be:
- Format: JPEG (.jpg)
- Recommended size: Similar to other logos (around 40-60KB)
- Dimensions: Suitable for web display (e.g., 200-400px width)
- Background: Preferably transparent or white

You can download the logo from:
- Official website: https://wittamer.com
- Or use a high-quality image from their branding materials

Once added, the logo will automatically appear in:
1. Business directory listings (/directory)
2. Map popup markers
3. Business detail pages
